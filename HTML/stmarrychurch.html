OVERVIEW
St. Mary's Basilica, located in the heart of Bangalore, Karnataka, is one of the oldest and most prominent churches in India. Established in the 17th century, this Gothic-style basilica is dedicated to St. Mary, the mother of Jesus Christ. Known for its stunning architecture, vibrant stained-glass windows, and serene atmosphere, the basilica serves as a major pilgrimage site, especially during the annual St. Mary's Feast in September, which attracts thousands of devotees from across the country. The basilica stands as a testament to Bangalore's rich cultural and religious diversity, offering a place of worship and tranquility amidst the bustling city.
