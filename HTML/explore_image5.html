OVERVIEW
     On the first day ,Arrival in Thekkady and transfer to the hotel or resort near Periyar National Park.Check-in and rest.Visit the nearby Spice Plantations to witness the      cultivation of various spices like cardamom, pepper, and cinnamon.Return to the hotel or resort for dinner and overnight stay

     On the second day, Breakfast at the hotel or resort then Enjoy a wildlife safari in Periyar National Park to witness the diverse wildlife, including elephants, tigers, and      various species of birds .Return to the hotel or resort for lunch. Enjoy a trekking expedition to the nearby hills to witness the stunning scenery and natural beauty of the       area.Return to the hotel or resort for dinner and overnight stay

     On the third day,Breakfast at the hotel or resort.Visit the nearby Mangala Devi Temple to witness the stunning architecture and learn about the history and culture of the      area. Return to the hotel or resort for lunch.Enjoy a boat ride on the Periyar Lake to witness the stunning scenery and local life along the way. Return to the hotel or resort      for dinner and overnight stay

     On the fourth day,Breakfast at the hotel or resort .Visit the nearby Elephant Junction to witness the various activities related to elephants like bathing, feeding, and riding.      Return to the hotel or resort for lunch. Visit the nearby Kalari Center to witness the ancient martial art of Kerala called Kalaripayattu. Return to the hotel or resort for      dinner and overnight stay

     On the fifth day,Breakfast at the hotel or resort. Check-out and departure from Thekkady.
