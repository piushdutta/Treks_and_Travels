OVERVIEW
Embark on an exhilarating journey with the Rishikesh Rafting and Kayaking Adventure Tour Package. This 7-night, 8-day adventure, priced at ₹25,000 per person (minimum 4 persons), offers a thrilling blend of water sports and serene andscapes. Upon arrival in Rishikesh, you will transfer to your hotel and enjoy a welcome dinner with a briefing about the exciting activities ahead. On Day 2, start your adventure with a session of rafting on the Ganges River, experiencing the thrill of navigating rapids amidst stunning natural surroundings. Return to the hotel for a relaxing evening. Day 3 is dedicated to kayaking, where you will paddle through calm and challenging waters, guided by professionals. Spend the evening exploring the local markets and temples. On Day 4, enjoy a combination of rafting and kayaking, honing your skills and soaking in the breathtaking scenery. Day 5 takes you to explore the nearby areas with a guided trek, offering a break from the water activities and a chance to appreciate the lush landscapes. Return to Rishikesh for the night. On Day 6, experience another day of rafting, focusing on more advanced rapids and techniques. Day 7 provides a leisure day to relax or engage in optional activities such as visiting the ashrams, participating in yoga sessions, or exploring the local sights. On the final day, check out from your hotel and transfer to the airport or railway station for your onward journey. The package includes comfortable accommodation, all specified adventure activities with professional guidance, local transportation, and meals as outlined. Exclusions cover airfare or transportation to/from Rishikesh, personal expenses, travel insurance, and any additional activities not listed.
