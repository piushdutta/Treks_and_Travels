ADI YOGI
The Source of Yoga
Adiyogi is located at the Isha Yoga Centre. Its height, 112 ft, symbolizes the 112 possibilities to attain to moksha (liberation) that are mentioned in yogic culture, and also the 112 chakras in the human system.
Ayodhya
The City of Ram
The Ram Mandir in Ayodhya is a Hindu temple complex recently inaugurated in January 2024. Believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama, it's a significant pilgrimage site for Hindus after a long-standing religious dispute.
Basilica of Bom Jesus
Catholic Basilica
The Basilica of Bom Jesus in Goa is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It's a stunning example of Baroque architecture and a pilgrimage site due to the enshrined remains of St. Francis Xavier.
Badarinath Temple
Perched high in the Himalayas, Badrinath Temple is a Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Vishnu, one of the four Dhams (holy pilgrimage sites) attracting devotees for centuries.

About Religious Tourism in India

India, a land of diverse spiritual traditions, welcomes you to its sacred temples and holy sites. Visit Varanasi, bathe in the Ganges, or find peace at the Golden Temple in Amritsar. Experience the tranquility of Bodh Gaya, where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment.

Your pilgrimage not only enriches your soul but also supports local economies. Enjoy local accommodations, savor traditional cuisines, and explore vibrant markets. Major events like the Kumbh Mela bring millions together, creating economic opportunities and fostering community development.
